❓ Frequently Asked Questions

I can't log in to my Pro account — what should I do?
Try resetting your password via the login page. If you still can't access your account, email us at gateresume@gmail.com with the email you used to purchase.
Can I use ResumeGate for free?
Yes — free users get 3 analyses per month. Pro unlocks unlimited scans, AI rewriting, and file export.
Is my resume data stored anywhere?
No. Resume content is processed temporarily and never saved. See our Privacy Policy for details.
What AI model powers ResumeGate?
We use Google Gemini API for analysis and rewriting. Your data is not used to train the model.
Can I get a refund?
Due to the digital nature of the product, all sales are final. If you have a technical issue, contact us within 7 days and we'll do our best to help.
