Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

👋 Overview

At ResumeGate, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. We aim to be transparent and straightforward.

📄 Resume & Document Data

We do not store your CV or resume content on our servers after analysis is complete. Uploaded text and PDFs are processed temporarily and immediately discarded once the analysis is returned to you.

Your resume content is never saved to a database, never linked to your account, and never used for any purpose other than generating your analysis results.

🤖 AI Processing (Google Gemini)

Resume analysis and rewriting is powered by the Google Gemini API. Your document content is sent to Google's servers for processing.

  • Google does not use your data to train their models (as per their API terms).
  • Gemini API data handling is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • We do not share your data with any other third-party AI services.

👤 Account Data (Pro Users)

If you purchase a Pro plan, we store account information to manage your subscription. This is handled via Supabase, a secure cloud database provider.

We may store:

  • Your email address (used for account identification)
  • Subscription status and plan type
  • Payment reference (from Lemon Squeezy)
  • Account creation date
Authentication is handled via email and password through Supabase Auth. Passwords are never stored in plaintext — they are hashed and managed securely by Supabase.

💳 Payments (Lemon Squeezy)

All payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy. We never see or store your card number, billing address, or payment details.

Lemon Squeezy's handling of payment data is governed by their own Privacy Policy.

🍪 Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to track your free scan usage (up to 3 per month). No cookies are set. No tracking pixels. No analytics.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for resumegate.vercel.app.

⚖️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Ask what data we hold about you
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at gateresume@gmail.com.

📬 Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at gateresume@gmail.com or visit our Contact page.
